[ 偏角の和と差 ] \begin{align} e^{i(\alpha+\beta)} &= \cos{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} - \sin{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta} + i(\sin{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} + \cos{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta}) \\ e^{i(\alpha-\beta)} &= \cos{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} + \sin{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta} + i(\sin{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} - \cos{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta}) \end{align} まとめると以下のように書ける \begin{align} e^{i(\alpha\pm\beta)} &= \cos{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} \mp \sin{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta} + i(\sin{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} \pm \cos{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta}) \end{align}
\begin{align} \cos(\alpha\pm\beta) &=\cos{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} \mp \sin{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta} \\ \sin(\alpha\pm\beta) &=\sin{\alpha} \cdot \cos{\beta} \pm \cos{\alpha} \cdot \sin{\beta} \end{align}
